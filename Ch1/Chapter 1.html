RECIPE FOR SOOBIN'S COOKIES

Welcome to Soobin's Cookies page ! We offer the best cookies in the world ^^

Below is the recipe for the delicious treat. I hope you made them with love and enjoyment.


INGREDIENTS

  • Butter (500g)
    • Room Temp.
  • White sugar (135g)
  • Brown sugar (125g)
  • Egg (2)
  • Vanilla Extract (2 tsp)
  • All purpose Flour (210g)
  • Baking powder (1 tsp)
  • Baking soda (1 tsp)
  • Salt (1 pinch)
  • Chocolate Chip (225g)
    • dark or milk chocolate would do


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Pre heat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Melt the butter on the microwave or using the heating pan for 30 minutes.
  3. Pour the melted butter in to the bowl together with the white and brown sugar and wisk until fluffly.
    Add the egg and vanilla exract then mix thoroughly.
  4. Sift the dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt) carefully and add it to the butter mixture until mixed well into a dough like texture. Then add the chocolate chips.
  5. Roll 18 to 20 balls from the dough, evenly place and flatten the dough a bit on the parchment paper.
  6. Put the baking tray inside the oven and bake for about 10 minutes.
  7. Allow the cookies to rest for a few mins then transfer to a wired rack to cool. Add a toppings if you like then enjoy your delicious cookies !
