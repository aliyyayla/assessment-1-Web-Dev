Overview

Andrea Sachs or Andy, a newly college graduate finds herself a job at the most powerful and influential magazine editor. She faces a nightmare to keep up with all her boss, Miranda Priestly's cynical demands and exausting schedules. Andy changes for the better as she adopted her environment from work, she was succeeding which turned her life upside down. Andy struggles between her desire to be a successful assistant in the fashion world and to live in a more fulfilling and authentic life.
